About Us

Coffee Beans - Enjoy the Aroma, Flavor, and Passion in Every Coffee Bean!

At Coffee Beans, we believe that every perfect cup of coffee starts with extraordinary coffee beans. We offer the finest selection of beans, carefully harvested from quality plantations and meticulously processed to preserve the authenticity of their flavor and aroma. Our commitment is to deliver an unforgettable coffee experience, straight from the farm to your cup. We collaborate with experienced local coffee farmers, ensuring that every bean we sell is processed sustainably, with respect for the environment and the well-being of the community. With a wide variety of Arabica, Robusta, and single-origin beans, we are dedicated to satisfying the taste of every coffee lover. Savor the journey of flavor with every sip. At Coffee Beans, coffee is more than just a drink – it’s tradition, joy, and the passion for sharing special moments.

Our Team

At Coffee Beans, our team is the heart of everything we do. Each member brings unique expertise and passion, ensuring that every step of the coffee-making journey is crafted with care and precision.

Nur Aziz Anandita
Aziz is the master behind our coffee’s distinctive flavors. With over a decade of experience, Aziz oversees the roasting process, carefully selecting and roasting each batch of beans to perfection. His deep understanding of coffee profiles ensures that every cup we offer is rich, balanced, and full of character.
Akfal Nasrullah
Akfal is the operational backbone of Coffee Beans, managing the logistics and ensuring that our beans get from the farm to your cup smoothly. From coordinating with farmers to overseeing quality control, Akfal eye for detail and organizational skills keep our processes running efficiently and sustainably.
Addin Ilyas
Addin is the voice of Coffee Beans. With a passion for storytelling and a love for coffee culture, Addin leads our marketing and sales efforts. His mission is to connect with coffee lovers everywhere, sharing the story of our beans and building long-lasting relationships with our customers.
